Dump Kernel Callstack

by Bramante - - | Comments

在Console Dump Kernel的Callstack很簡單, 只要執行下列的Command即可印出所有Task的Callstack:

echo 1 > /proc/sys/kernel/sysrq
echo 9 > /proc/sys/kernel/printk
echo "t" > /proc/sysrq-trigger

如果Hot Key有用的話, 也可以用Hot Key觸發:

echo 1 > /proc/sys/kernel/sysrq
echo 9 > /proc/sys/kernel/printk
Ctrl + break  放開再按  t
